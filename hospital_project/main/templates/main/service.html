Services

Health Care Solutions

Cardiology

Our Cardiology department offers comprehensive heart care, including diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of heart-related conditions. From routine check-ups to advanced procedures, our expert cardiologists provide personalized care to ensure your heart health is in the best hands.

Pulmonary

The Pulmonary department at CarePoint specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of respiratory diseases, such as asthma, COPD (Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease), pneumonia, and lung cancer. Our team of pulmonologists and respiratory therapists offer expert care to help you breathe easier.

Neurology

Our Neurology department offers specialized care for disorders of the brain, spinal cord, and nervous system. From diagnosing and treating conditions such as epilepsy, migraines, and multiple sclerosis, to managing neurodegenerative diseases, our neurologists provide compassionate and cutting-edge care.

Orthopedics

The Orthopedics department at CarePoint provides expert care for musculoskeletal disorders, from sports injuries to joint replacements. Our orthopedic surgeons specialize in both surgical and non-surgical treatments for conditions affecting the bones, muscles, ligaments, and joints.

Dental Surgery

At CarePoint, our Dental Surgery department provides a wide range of surgical treatments aimed at improving your oral health. From routine tooth extractions to more complex oral surgeries, our experienced dental surgeons utilize advanced techniques to ensure you receive the best care.

Laboratory

Our state-of-the-art laboratory offers a wide range of diagnostic services to help identify and monitor various medical conditions. With the latest technology and highly trained lab technicians, we ensure accurate results and fast turnarounds.
